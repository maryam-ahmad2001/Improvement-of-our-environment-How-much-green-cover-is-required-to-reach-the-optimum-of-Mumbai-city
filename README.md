# Improvement-of-our-environment-How-much-green-cover-is-required-to-reach-the-optimum-of-Mumbai-city
Research Paper- Improvement of our environment: How much green cover is required to reach the optimum of Mumbai city and how can we reach it?

Our aim was to find out how much percentage of green cover Mumbai had compared to the total area of the city and what we could do to make it reach 15 percent of the city area. We gathered the needful data and reached a conclusion. Furthermore, we did a detailed study on various trees and which ones proved to be the best fit considering the climate and area constraints.
